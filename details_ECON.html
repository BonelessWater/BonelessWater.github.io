Project information

  • Category: Data Analysis
  • Project date: March, 2024
  • Project URL: https://github.com/BonelessWater/Unemployment_Rate_Agregate

Inspiration

When writing a final paper on the effects of the housing crisis and gentrification on the San Francisco Bay Area, my teacher instructed me to make graphs with user-friendly software we used throught the course. However, these software did not supply the necessary granularity and accuracy I needed to persuade the reader. This need brought me to making my own script to accomplish this task.

Explaination

This graph is composed of all unemployment data in the US organized county from 2000 to 2022 collected by the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics. It compared the unemployment rate in the US to counties in the Bay Area to discribe the effect the housing crisis had on the Bay Area.

Takeaways

This mini-project taught me methods of parsing foreign data and the power of visual discriptions to tell a story.
